Overview

FractalX ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect on this website, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

This site is a pre-launch landing page for the FractalX platform — a Java microservices framework. The only personal data we collect is your email address if you choose to sign up for early access notifications.

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary:

  • Email address — submitted voluntarily via the early access sign-up form. We do not collect names, payment details, or any other personal information at this stage.
  • Usage data — standard server logs may include your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This data is not linked to your email address and is used only for diagnosing issues and understanding aggregate traffic.
How We Use Your Information

Your email address is used exclusively to:

  • Notify you when FractalX launches or early access becomes available
  • Send occasional product updates relevant to the launch

We will never sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage & Security

Email addresses are stored securely and access is restricted to the FractalX team. We apply reasonable technical measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Opt out of any communications by replying to any email we send you
  • Request a copy of the information we hold about you

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.
