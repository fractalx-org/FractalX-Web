What FractalX Enterprise will offer

We're designing the enterprise tier around real teams migrating real monoliths. Here's what's planned.

Priority Support
Direct access to the core team with guaranteed response times. Issues get escalated immediately — no ticket queues.
Architecture Reviews
One-on-one sessions to analyse your current monolith structure and map the optimal decomposition strategy for your system.
Team Training
Workshops and onboarding sessions tailored to your engineering team — from annotations to advanced decomposition patterns.
Migration Consulting
Hands-on guidance throughout your migration. We help plan rollout, handle edge cases, and keep your services stable in production.
Custom Integrations
Need FractalX to fit your internal CI/CD or deployment platform? We work with you to build the connectors you need.
Roadmap Influence
Enterprise partners get direct input into the FractalX roadmap. Your use cases shape the features we prioritise next.
